Pending full-text extraction. This longitudinal twin-difference study examines the relationship between parenting practices and children's ADHD symptoms, leveraging the twin design to control for genetic confounds.

## Clinical Insight
Twin-difference designs that isolate environmental parenting effects from genetic transmission are valuable for clinicians working with families of children with ADHD, as they help identify which parenting strategies are most likely to produce behavioral change independently of genetic risk.
