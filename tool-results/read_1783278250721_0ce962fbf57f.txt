Second, I review the evidence the paper uses to support its claim that there is little evidence for configural invariance, highlighting problems and errors in the analyses and reporting of results. Third, I review the evidence presented against the presence of Conscientiousness in chimpanzees and find it problematic. Finally, I demonstrate that the literature review lacks due care.
