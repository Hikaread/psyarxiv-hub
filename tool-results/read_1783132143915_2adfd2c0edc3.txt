   207вҶ’Statistical Approach
   208вҶ’24
   209вҶ’25
   210вҶ’26 This study employs multi-group structural equation modeling (MGSEM) to test for
   211вҶ’27 tenability of equality constraints/additional parameters estimated across language
   212вҶ’28 groups in an National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent to Adult Health (ADD) Health
   213вҶ’29
   214вҶ’sample. Configural invariance examines whether the factor structure is consistent
   215вҶ’30
   216вҶ’across groups, while metric invariance imposes equality of factor loadings, and scalar
   217вҶ’31
   218вҶ’32 invariance evaluates relative tenability of holding intercepts equal (Meredith, 1993). To
   219вҶ’33 evaluate the structural relationships, we will test whether paths between constructs
   220вҶ’34
   221вҶ’estimated reliably and receptive vocabulary are equivalent across groups (or better
   222вҶ’35
   223вҶ’estimated freely), using robust maximum likelihood estimation (MLR) to ease
   224вҶ’36
   225вҶ’multivariate normality assumptions. We consider the guidelines outlined by Chen
   226вҶ’37
   227вҶ’38 (2007), wherein statistical thresholds for model retention may include О”CFI < .01 and
   228вҶ’39 О”RMSEA < .015, as metric and other equality constraints are evaluated. By establishing
   229вҶ’40
   230вҶ’invariance concerning certain parameters, this research aims for the parsimonious
   231вҶ’41
   232вҶ’modeling of unmeasured health & lifestyle constructs to receptive vocabulary
   233вҶ’42
   234вҶ’43 performance. Generally, when the constraint of additional parameters resulted in little to
   235вҶ’44 no degradation in model fit (or improvement), the simpler model with fewer parameters
   236вҶ’45
   237вҶ’is preferred.

   606вҶ’32 Discussion
   607вҶ’33
   608вҶ’34
   609вҶ’The notion that people experiencing depression may perform more poorly than those
   610вҶ’35
   611вҶ’36 not experiencing depression is not suprising in itself. Regarding the general notion of
   612вҶ’37 the depressive impact on relevant cognitive abilities, Henry & Crawford (2005)
   613вҶ’38
   614вҶ’corroborate, concluding their meta-analysis with the argument that both phonemic and
   615вҶ’39
   616вҶ’semantic fluency are sensitive to the influence of depression not due to degradation of
   617вҶ’40
   618вҶ’41 executive function or the semantic store, but rather because of a more generalized
   619вҶ’42 cognitive slowing. Intra/inter-assessment-level data would be required to make intra-
   620вҶ’43 ability/inter-ability distinctions here, however, the empirical findings are in agreement
   621вҶ’44
   622вҶ’with the performance expectations of other neurocognitive research. While the influence
   623вҶ’45
   624вҶ’of other factors were not left in the model (or statistically non-zero influences) for the
   625вҶ’46
   626вҶ’47 sample at hand, sleep (insomnia), mood, and alcohol were previously shown in other
   627вҶ’48 work to notably impact cognitive abilities relevant to the receptive vocabulary task used
   628вҶ’49
   629вҶ’here as the outcome. Moreover, it should not be understated that the typical
   630вҶ’50
   631вҶ’administration of receptive vocabulary tasks is with each trial, first an audible
   632вҶ’51
   633вҶ’52 presentation of a target word in English - followed by visual depictions of the word (to
   634вҶ’53 match) in 4-picture array presentations. Bialystok & Luk (2011) also used receptive
   635вҶ’54 vocabulary as an outcome for study across language groups of participants
   636вҶ’55
   637вҶ’(monolinguals and bilinguals), finding significant differences between the monolingual
   638вҶ’56
   639вҶ’and bilingual groups, F(1, 1603) = 138.4, p < .0001. Participants in the ADD Health
   640вҶ’57
   641вҶ’58 Study were deemed proficient with regard to English and such determinations appear to
   642вҶ’59 suggest tenability of the suggestion for equal sensitivity to the effects of depression in
   643вҶ’60
   644вҶ’61
   645вҶ’62
   646вҶ’63
   647вҶ’64
   648вҶ’65
   649вҶ’
   650вҶ’1
   651вҶ’2
   652вҶ’3
   653вҶ’4 the analysis sample of participants. Still, it may be unwise to consider the typically
   654вҶ’5
   655вҶ’audible recognition of the English target word (during Peabody-like Vocabulary tasks)
   656вҶ’6
   657вҶ’as universal presentations regardless of the illustrated nature of the rest of the task in a
   658вҶ’7
   659вҶ’8 given trial. The leverage of first-language adaptive receptive vocabulary tasks may
   660вҶ’9 (even) better remove the potential biases, aligning participants with the initial language
   661вҶ’10
   662вҶ’in which the target of each trial of the task is administered. Further research with such a
   663вҶ’11
   664вҶ’modification may provide suitable models inclusive of other lifestyle/health factors for
   665вҶ’12
   666вҶ’research across cultures. The view is taken that the processes underlying facets
   667вҶ’13
   668вҶ’14 involved in recognition-maintenance-matching trials and comparable everyday demands
   669вҶ’15 may be compromised by the influence of depression.
