   596вЖТ   596вЖТ24
   597вЖТ   597вЖТ25
   598вЖТ   598вЖТThe data support a structural invariance model in which the negative impact of

   600вЖТ   600вЖТdepression on receptive vocabulary performance is estimated to be pronounced

   603вЖТ   603вЖТ29 and English participants.

   605вЖТ   605вЖТ31
   606вЖТ   606вЖТ32 Discussion
   607вЖТ   607вЖТ33
   608вЖТ   608вЖТ34
   609вЖТ   609вЖТThe notion that people experiencing depression may perform more poorly than those
   610вЖТ   610вЖТ35
   611вЖТ   611вЖТ36 not experiencing depression is not suprising in itself. Regarding the general notion of
   612вЖТ   612вЖТ37 the depressive impact on relevant cognitive abilities, Henry & Crawford (2005)
   613вЖТ   613вЖТ38
   614вЖТ   614вЖТcorroborate, concluding their meta-analysis with the argument that both phonemic and
   615вЖТ   615вЖТ39
   616вЖТ   616вЖТsemantic fluency are sensitive to the influence of depression not due to degradation of
   617вЖТ   617вЖТ40
   618вЖТ   618вЖТ41 executive function or the semantic store, but rather because of a more generalized
   619вЖТ   619вЖТ42 cognitive slowing. Intra/inter-assessment-level data would be required to make intra-
   620вЖТ   620вЖТ43 ability/inter-ability distinctions here, however, the empirical findings are in agreement
   621вЖТ   621вЖТ44
   622вЖТ   622вЖТwith the performance expectations of other neurocognitive research. While the influence
   623вЖТ   623вЖТ45
   624вЖТ   624вЖТof other factors were not left in the model (or statistically non-zero influences) for the
   625вЖТ   625вЖТ46
   626вЖТ   626вЖТ47 sample at hand, sleep (insomnia), mood, and alcohol were previously shown in other
   627вЖТ   627вЖТ48 work to notably impact cognitive abilities relevant to the receptive vocabulary task used
   628вЖТ   628вЖТ49
   629вЖТ   629вЖТhere as the outcome. Moreover, it should not be understated that the typical
   630вЖТ   630вЖТ50
   631вЖТ   631вЖТadministration of receptive vocabulary tasks is with each trial, first an audible
   632вЖТ   632вЖТ51
   633вЖТ   633вЖТ52 presentation of a target word in English - followed by visual depictions of the word (to
   634вЖТ   634вЖТ53 match) in 4-picture array presentations. Bialystok & Luk (2011) also used receptive
   635вЖТ   635вЖТ54 vocabulary as an outcome for study across language groups of participants
   636вЖТ   636вЖТ55
   637вЖТ   637вЖТ(monolinguals and bilinguals), finding significant differences between the monolingual
   638вЖТ   638вЖТ56
   639вЖТ   639вЖТand bilingual groups, F(1, 1603) = 138.4, p < .0001. Participants in the ADD Health
   640вЖТ   640вЖТ57
   641вЖТ   641вЖТ58 Study were deemed proficient with regard to English and such determinations appear to
   642вЖТ   642вЖТ59 suggest tenability of the suggestion for equal sensitivity to the effects of depression in
   643вЖТ   643вЖТ60
   644вЖТ   644вЖТ61
   645вЖТ   645вЖТ62
   646вЖТ   646вЖТ63
   647вЖТ   647вЖТ64
   648вЖТ   648вЖТ65
   649вЖТ   649вЖТ
   650вЖТ   650вЖТ1
   651вЖТ   651вЖТ2
   652вЖТ   652вЖТ3
   653вЖТ   653вЖТ4 the analysis sample of participants. Still, it may be unwise to consider the typically
   654вЖТ   654вЖТ5
   655вЖТ   655вЖТaudible recognition of the English target word (during Peabody-like Vocabulary tasks)
   656вЖТ   656вЖТ6
   657вЖТ   657вЖТas universal presentations regardless of the illustrated nature of the rest of the task in a
   658вЖТ   658вЖТ7
   659вЖТ   659вЖТ8 given trial. The leverage of first-language adaptive receptive vocabulary tasks may
   660вЖТ   660вЖТ9 (even) better remove the potential biases, aligning participants with the initial language
   661вЖТ   661вЖТ10
   662вЖТ   662вЖТin which the target of each trial of the task is administered. Further research with such a
   663вЖТ   663вЖТ11
   664вЖТ   664вЖТmodification may provide suitable models inclusive of other lifestyle/health factors for
   665вЖТ   665вЖТ12
   666вЖТ   666вЖТresearch across cultures. The view is taken that the processes underlying facets
   667вЖТ   667вЖТ13
   668вЖТ   668вЖТ14 involved in recognition-maintenance-matching trials and comparable everyday demands
   669вЖТ   669вЖТ15 may be compromised by the influence of depression.
